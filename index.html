Knowledge Cutoff

Practical AI for people with actual jobs.

Everything you know about AI is already out of date. AI changes every week. This is a newsletter about using AI in real world on real work without the hype cycle, prompt theater or vague promises.

One useful AI-at-work field note. No spam. Unsubscribe anytime.

What it is

A practical newsletter for operators, nonprofit staff, digital teams, and small organizations trying to make AI useful on Monday morning.

What it is not

Not model news, not prompt lists, not breathless futurism, and not advice from someone whose only job is writing about AI.

What you get

Specific field notes from actual work: what helped, what broke, what needed checking, and what is worth trying for yourself.
